Mental Point

This apps is a collboration between the Canadian Mental Health Association





Survey is marked 1 to 5, 1 being lowest, 5 being the highest

Generally, how pleased with life have you been feeling today?

1 - Not Pleased At All 2 - A Little Pleased 3 - Somewhat Pleased 4 - Pleased 5 - Really Pleased at Life
